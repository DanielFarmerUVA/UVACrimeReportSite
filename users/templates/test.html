Welcome to the UVA Crime Report site. This platform is designed for the easy and prompt reporting of crimes within the University of Virginia community, fostering a safer and more aware campus environment.

For crimes in progress or crimes that just occurred, or in an emergency, call 911. The dispatcher can send police, fire and medical rescue.

In a non-emergency only, call 434 924-7166.

If you have additional questions about this specific reporting process, feel free to send us an email at a03.host@gmail.com.
